SurveySeed.AI

November 29, 2023 • 1 min read • by renerocksai

AI-utomating an entire industry with a GPT-4-based market research agent

Are AI Agents the future of Market Research? At NIM, I developed a fully automated prototype agent with a GPT brain that produces insights and concrete, actionable results from a single query by designing and running online surveys with real participants, and then analyzing the data, incl. open-ended survey responses.

There’s also a “how it works” at the end of the video below.

This is one of the research projects where I used a Zap backend, to much success. It featured endpoints for survey creation and monitoring by the agent, the frontend web app for interfacing with the agent, and hosted all the surveys conducted by the agent.
