Eye-contact with social robots

September 22, 2023 • 1 min read • by renerocksai

The impact of eye-contact with social robots...

We put the robo into roboadvisors for investment advice - and guess what happened? Its ability to maintain vs. avoid eyecontact had a similar effect on trust when compared to a human advisor.

Read more about it at NIM.org, my former employer.

Simulating hundreds of virtual online meetings in parallel brought our server to its limits and sparked the creation of Zap.
